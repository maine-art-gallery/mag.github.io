Moving Forward in a Pandemic Year
There was an air of excitement during the early 2020 meetings of the Maine Art Gallery Board of Directors. Plans for summer exhibits were well under way and we were developing plans for fundraising and promotion. Wendy Ross and Tom Eichler had agreed to head up the fundraising. Chris Hopf-Lovette was supervising ad production and placement, and John Gable, an artist who would be participating in the Contemporary Realism show met with us to share his ideas about attracting more visitors to the gallery. And then, in the latter part of March, the Coronavirus brought in-person activities to a halt. The gallery would have to remain shuttered for the season. But thanks to the initiative and technical skills of our webmaster, Elizabeth Campbell, the shows have gone ahead as virtual online exhibits. With her help we are also taking this opportunity to reach out and invite more visitors to our website, www.maineartgallerywiscasset.org. We invite you to visit www.maineartgallerywiscasset,org to view our exhibits, keep up with our activities, and make a donation online to help us reach our goal of funding a part-time staff member.
